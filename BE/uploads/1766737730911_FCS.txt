---Admin Side---

Tenant Management:
 -Create and manage tenants with isolated branding, configurations, and data storage.
 -Enable scalability by adding tenants seamlessly.

Notification Management:
 -Configure SMS and email notifications for reminders, campaigns, etc.
 -View delivery reports, open rates, and customer interactions.
 -Integrate notification services like Twilio or AWS SES.

Payment Management:
 -Configure tenant-specific payment fields (e.g., customer details, amount, offers).
 -Manage payment gateway providers for secure transactions.

Response Management:
 -Predefine replies for common customer queries.
 -Allow manual intervention for personalized service.

System Monitoring:
 -Track admin activities via an audit trail.
 -Monitor webhook events for real-time system updates.

Reports and Analytics:
 -Generate reports on notifications, payments, and system performance.

---User Side----

Payment Landing Page:
Show customer-specific details like balances, offers, and history.
Support secure tokenized payments with multiple options (full/partial).

Notification Preferences:
Enable users to manage email and SMS reminders for payments and offers.

Quick Actions:
Pre-configured replies for common queries.
Access to offers and create payment plans.

Self-Service Options:
Manage personal account details and notification preferences.
